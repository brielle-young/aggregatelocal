Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer delivery or pickup options?

Aggregate provides both delivery and pickup options, depending on the preferences of the individual producers. Customers can choose the most convenient option when placing their orders.

How does the ordering process work?

Customers can browse through the products listed by farmers on our platform, select the items they wish to purchase, and complete their order online. The farmers then fulfill the orders and arrange for delivery or pickup options.

How can I join Aggregate as a producer?

We welcome all small-scale farmers who are interested in selling their products on our platform. Apply today to become a vendor through our website.

How do you ensure the quality of the products?

We work closely with our farmers to ensure that all products listed on our platform meet our quality standards. We also encourage feedback from customers to continuously improve the quality of offerings.

What kind of products can I find on Aggregate?

We offer a variety of products from small-scale farmers, including fresh produce, dairy products, meats, grains, and artisanal goods.

How does Aggregate benefit small-scale farmers?

Aggregate allows small-scale farmers to sell their products directly to customers, cutting out middlemen and increasing their profits. Additionally, it provides them with a wider audience and helps grow their businesses.
